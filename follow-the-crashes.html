A template for exploring Thai public datasets, one story at a time. Data model mirrors the BMA 100-cluster risk map. To go live, see the LIVE_CONFIG block in source and set enabled = true. Methodology: severity is bucketed by fatality count (fatal ≥5, major 2–4, minor 0–1); casualty radius encodes √(d·3 + i).
